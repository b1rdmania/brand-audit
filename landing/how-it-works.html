Under the hood

The tech nerd bit

You don't need to understand any of this. But if you're curious about what's actually happening when we audit and fix your online presence, here's the machinery.

claude api

AI-powered auditing

Our scripts crawl your website, check platform APIs, and pull PageSpeed data. Claude analyses the raw data and produces scored findings with evidence — the same way a senior consultant would, except it checks hundreds of signals in minutes.

json-ld / open graph

Structured data & schema

When ChatGPT answers a question about your industry, it pulls from structured data. Most small business websites have none. We generate Product, LocalBusiness, Organization, and FAQ schema and inject it directly into your site.

llm evaluation

AI discoverability scoring

Traditional SEO measures Google rankings. We also test whether AI tools can accurately describe your business, recommend you, and cite your products. If an LLM can't find enough context, you're invisible to a growing share of search.

nlp pipeline

Voice extraction

Your existing copy gets profiled for sentence structure, vocabulary, tone, and rhythm. The output is a voice model that ensures generated content matches how you actually write, not generic AI copy.

shopify / google / pinterest api

Platform automation

We use the Shopify Admin API to rewrite descriptions in place, the Google Business Profile API to create your listing, and platform-specific integrations for Pinterest, Trustpilot, and directories. Where APIs exist, we use them.

competitive intelligence

Competitor analysis

We run the same scoring pipeline against 3–5 businesses in your space, identify where they're beating you on discoverability, and prioritise your fixes based on the gaps that matter most.
